Summary: PassCard does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your passes are created locally on your device and stored in Apple Wallet.

Introduction

PassCard ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do Not Collect

PassCard is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any content from your passes

How the App Works

PassCard allows you to create digital passes for Apple Wallet. Here's how your data is handled:

  • Pass Creation: When you create a pass, the information you enter (event name, date, venue, etc.) is processed locally on your device or through our secure server to generate an Apple Wallet pass.
  • Apple Wallet: Generated passes are added directly to your Apple Wallet and stored by Apple according to their privacy policy.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your passes or personal information on any servers.

Server Communication

The app communicates with our server solely to generate properly signed Apple Wallet passes. This process:

  • Is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Does not store any pass data after generation
  • Does not log personal information
  • Does not use cookies or tracking

Third-Party Services

PassCard does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store personal data, there is no risk of data breaches affecting your personal information through our app.

Children's Privacy

PassCard does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app is suitable for all ages.
