Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a pass?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen, fill in the details for your event, ticket, or card, and tap "Add to Wallet" to save it directly to Apple Wallet.

Can I edit a pass after creating it?

Yes! Tap on any saved pass in the app, then tap the "Edit" button to modify its details. The updated pass will be synced to your Apple Wallet.

Where are my passes stored?

Your passes are stored securely in Apple Wallet on your device. PassCard does not store your pass data on any external servers.

Can I use PassCard on multiple devices?

Passes added to Apple Wallet will sync across your devices through iCloud if you have iCloud enabled for Wallet.

What types of passes can I create?

PassCard supports Event Tickets, Boarding Passes, Coupons, Store Cards, and Generic passes. Choose the type that best fits your needs.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! We don't collect or store any personal data. All processing happens on your device, and passes go directly to Apple Wallet. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

The app shows "Server Error"

Make sure you have an active internet connection. If the problem persists, try going to Settings in the app and checking the server connection status.

PassCard is a simple and elegant way to create custom passes for Apple Wallet. Whether you need event tickets, membership cards, or coupons, PassCard makes it easy to create beautiful passes in seconds.
